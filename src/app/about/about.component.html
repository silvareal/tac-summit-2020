TAC Africa Summit 2020

will be organized under the patronage of the Office of the National Security Adviser (ONSA) and the Ministry of Aviation in partnership with International organization and other Authorized Agencies.

Overall, the Summit is hoped to bring together a High-level conference with comparative views of industry leaders, government officials, researchers from national and international subject matter expertise a national compendium of Standard operation Procedure and International Best Practices for Drone & Counter Drone operations is envisaged to be built from this working document.
