OVERVIEW

This Lab will bring together global subject matter experts from Academia, Law Enforcement Agencies, industry expert and all concerned stakeholders through a dedicated online Platform to chart a global cause for Africa and the world at large to prepare a successful hosting plan for the Summit.

The submissions from this lab will also include but are not limited to producing a global compendium of drone related subjects that will be published and made available to both regional and international communities to steer a discuss that will be on the front burner for this Summit.

Organization Committe

TAC Africa Drone & Counter-Drone organizing committee for the 2020 summit will consist of 5 entities

Advisory Board

This is not yet another “think tank” but a group of experts that share in our vision to make Africa region and the world Safer.

Scientific Commitee

The Scientific Committee will be the coordinating head of discuss in the different co-creation groups.

Subject Matter Experts

This is the engine house that will create momentum for the implementation of global platform that will discuss drone related subject matter at a global level.

Secretariat

The Secretariat will maintain an overview of ongoing virtual platform discuss between the co-creation programmes and circulates the results or information as and at when due.

Industry Partners

Partners are organizations with specific Drone Expertise which are invited by the TAC Secretariat to participate in the initiative.
